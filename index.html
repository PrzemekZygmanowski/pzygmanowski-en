more about me

From Łódź by origin and birth. Nice boy from the University of Technology. The mind of an engineer makes me approach each task very methodically, and the search for solutions drives me to work. I successfully combine my passion for logic and analysis with a creative approach - this approach works both in designing installations and being a developer.

For 10 years I have been associated with the construction and sanitary industry. What does this have to do with programming? More than it may seem :) Thanks to the work of an engineer, I know perfectly well how to run projects and meet the client's needs. In my current job, as in the developer's job, accuracy and attention to detail are what counts. I am also a seasoned person when it comes to hours spent in front of the computer.

The work of an engineer did not pose new challenges for me, so I started to expand my knowledge, relying on the competences that I use on a daily basis. And that's how Przemek 2.0 - web weveloper was created!

web developing

From engineer to webdeveloper ... how did it start? A strict, analytical mind looking for a new industry. Approaching the matter logically, I focused on internet analytics. I got to know Google Analytics Academy and got the Google Analytics Individual Qualification certificate.

To become a good analyst, it's worth knowing the basics of programming, so I decided to dig deeper into the topic. And then magic happened! The more I studied, the more I wanted to know. The appetite grows with eating, so I didn't just focus on html basics. For the last year, I could not tear myself away from subsequent courses and implementing my own projects. All this to finally start a job that gives me real joy.

what I have been doing so far?

HVAC Instalations

I specialize in the following areas of the installation industry

heating instalations

I designed heating installations for multi-family buildings, production halls, hotels and service buildings. I also designed boiler rooms, heating nodes and heat networks.

Gas instalations

I have experience in the design of gas networks, 1st and 2nd stage gas reduction stations, connections and gas installations.

cooling installations

I designed precision air conditioning installations for the server rooms of the largest mobile network operators in Poland.

I have implemented projects for both private investors and the largest construction companies in Poland. In my portfolio you can find installations created, among others, for server rooms of one of the largest mobile network operators in Poland.

However, the work of an engineer is not only about design. I know how to create a working installation, but also how to plan work and lead a team so that no deadline will surprise me.

The computer is my inseparable work tool. Apart from Visual studio code, I do not close programs such as:

autocad

The greatest joy for me is to present sources of heat, cold and other complicated installations by means of technical drawings.

Instaltherm

Another basic software, thanks to which I can quickly calculate the heat demand for a building, and help me calculate the designed heating and cooling installations.

Profil koordynator

Thanks to the knowledge of the Profil Koordynator, I am able to draw a profile of the designed networks and connections, as well as to solve collisions and proximity to the existing underground infrastructure.
